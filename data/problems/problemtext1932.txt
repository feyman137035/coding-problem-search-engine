Given a strings, partition the string into one or moresubstringssuch that the characters in each substring areunique. That is, no letter appears in a single substring more thanonce.Returntheminimumnumber of substrings in such a partition.Note that each character should belong to exactly one substring in a partition.Example 1:Input:s = "abacaba"Output:4Explanation:Two possible partitions are ("a","ba","cab","a") and ("ab","a","ca","ba").It can be shown that 4 is the minimum number of substrings needed.Example 2:Input:s = "ssssss"Output:6Explanation:The only valid partition is ("s","s","s","s","s","s").Constraints:1 <= s.length <= 105sconsists of only English lowercase letters.
