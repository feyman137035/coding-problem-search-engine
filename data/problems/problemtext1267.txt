For example, round 1 starts at sectorrounds[0]and ends at sectorrounds[1]Returnan array of the most visited sectorssorted inascendingorder.Notice that you circulate the track in ascending order of sector numbers in the counter-clockwise direction (See the first example).Example 1:Input:n = 4, rounds = [1,3,1,2]Output:[1,2]Explanation:The marathon starts at sector 1.
