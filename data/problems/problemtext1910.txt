Thus, there are 19 special integers.Example 2:Input:n = 5Output:5Explanation:All the integers from 1 to 5 are special.Example 3:Input:n = 135Output:110Explanation:There are 110 integers from 1 to 135 that are special.Some of the integers that are not special are: 22, 114, and 131.Constraints:1 <= n <= 2 * 109
