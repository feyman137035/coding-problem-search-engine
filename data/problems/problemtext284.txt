After a tour, the smart thief realized that all houses in this place form a binary tree. It will automatically contact the police iftwo directly-linked houses were broken into on the same night.Given therootof the binary tree, returnthe maximum amount of money the thief can robwithout alerting the police.Example 1:Input:root = [3,2,3,null,3,null,1]Output:7Explanation:Maximum amount of money the thief can rob = 3 + 3 + 1 = 7.Example 2:Input:root = [3,4,5,1,3,null,1]Output:9Explanation:Maximum amount of money the thief can rob = 4 + 5 = 9.Constraints:The number of nodes in the tree is in the range[1, 104].0 <= Node.val <= 104
